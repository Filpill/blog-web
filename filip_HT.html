Heat Transfer Simulation with Finite Differencing Method and Implemented with Python

Personal project to re-write heat transfer simulation from MATLAB to Python. Solved using Successive Over Relaxation Algorithm and Finite Differnce Method used to capture incremental changes of temeperature on grid.
