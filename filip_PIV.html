An Experimental Study of Flow Sepration on a Forward Facing Step Using PIV

My elected project for my 3rd year in University was to design a Forward Facing Step and perform wind tunnel experiments to analyse flow characteristics on that type of geometry using PIV.

Particle Image Velocimetry is a practical tool for Aerodynamics analyse flow fields without intrusions in the wind tunnel environment. The method of extracting the data involved illuminating a plane of airflow with a laser and caputuring the images with a high speed camera. The images are then post processed with a program to obtain the velocity field.
